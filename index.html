To start a video call, please log in below.

To start a new chat room:

1) Click the hamburger icon on the top left side.

2) Click "Rooms"

3) Click "Create Room"

4) Fill out the Room name, privacy, etc

5) Click "Create room"

----------------------------------------------

How to share your video conference room's link:

1) Click the hamburger icon on the top left side.

2) Click "Rooms"

3) Under "Your rooms", click on the room you want to link to

4) Copy & paste the link under "URL"
